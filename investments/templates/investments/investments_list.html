About Investing

Investing involves allocating money to assets with the expectation of income or profit. Diversifying can help manage risk.

  • Stocks: Ownership in a company with dividend and growth potential.
  • Bonds: Loans with fixed interest.
  • Real Estate: Property investment offering rental income and appreciation.
  • Gold: A hedge against inflation.
  • ETFs: Asset collections trading like stocks, offering flexibility.
